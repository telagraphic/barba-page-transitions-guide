Animations

When doing animations that involve x and y coordinate changes, you will get scrollbars during the animation. Click on the navigation to see this in action.

To avoid this, uncomment out line 4 in scss/globals/_main.scss. When starting out with animations, you might not know this! overflow-x: hidden will keep the browser from displaying horizontal scrollbars during the animation.

Check the resources page on the master branch for otther animation libraries.

Easings

If using GSAP, check out their handy ease visualizer. Look at some of the easings used on transition timelines to give a smoother feel. These are basic boring animations, nothing great. But some easing smooths them out.

Timeline Position

Try using the position parameter to layer your to blocks over each other for even better animations. Timing out the fade before it reaches the end will make animations look less abrupt and blocky. This is another great video on relative and absolute timing.
