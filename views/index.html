Sync Mode

Sync mode enables you play enter and leave hooks at the same time. The idea being that you can have better control over the transition timing.

This repo by karanmhatre1 shows sync mode used with GSAP transitions. Here is a cool video that riffs on the same idea.

These are simple examples and might be great for simple small sites.
